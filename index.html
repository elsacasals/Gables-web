The Location

Coral Gables.
A City Apart.

Coral Gables isn't just a business address — it's one of South Florida's most distinguished communities. With its Mediterranean architecture, tree-lined boulevards, and vibrant culture, it draws the region's top professionals and institutions.

01

Miracle Mile & Giralda Plaza

Coral Gables offers a lively and well-rounded environment just steps away. Miracle Mile and Giralda Plaza feature a mix of restaurants, boutiques, wine bars, and specialty shops, complemented by cultural venues like theatres, cinemas, and nearby fitness and sports centers — making it an ideal setting for both work and lifestyle.

02

The Business District

Coral Gables is home to 40+ multinational corporations, a dozen consulates, and hundreds of leading law, finance, and advisory firms. The address communicates stability and prestige.

03

Le Jeune Road — A Prestigious Address

Le Jeune Road is one of Coral Gables' most distinguished thoroughfares. Being at 2655 Le Jeune puts your company at the crossroads of accessibility and distinction — an address clients remember.
